About Future Institute of Engineering and Management,Sonarpur

Two decades and more of enriching journey for Future Institute of Engineering and Management.Future Institute of Engineering and Management, also known as FIEM, is a private institution and a part of Team Future. The institute was established in 2002 at Sonarpur, Kolkata. It is approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) and is affiliated to Maulana Abul Kalam Azad University of Technology (MAKAUT).

Currently, the institute offers BTech, BCA, BBA, MCA and MBA programmes under various specialisations. It also offers BSc and MSc in Media Science under its constituent institute Future Media School. It also offers BBA in Hospital Management and MHA under the Future School of Hospital Management. These programmes are offered under ten different departments, namely Basic Science & Technology, Information Technology, Mechanical Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Electronics & Communication and more.

It also offers a Campus Connect Programme in association with Infosys. It is also associated with Wadhwani Foundation – NEN (National Entrepreneurship Network). FIEM also provides international opportunities through several development programmes in association with foreign universities such as North Dakota State University. Apart from this, it has placement accreditations with top organisations, including CTS, Capgemini, Infosys, ITC Infotech, TCS, Wipro Technologies and more.
